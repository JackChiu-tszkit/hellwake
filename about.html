About

JackVenture is an independent app development practice. We build mobile applications that are useful, clear, and respectful of your time and privacy.

What we do

We focus on three areas:

  • AI and productivity – Apps that use modern AI to help with tasks, organization, and learning.
  • Mindfulness and spirituality – Apps for meditation, reflection, and personal growth.
  • Daily life and wellness – Practical tools for routines, habits, health, and everyday challenges.

Our approach

We believe technology should support real life, not replace it. Our apps aim to be simple, reliable, and privacy-conscious. Where possible, we keep data on your device and avoid unnecessary tracking or clutter.
